[Achievements]
────────────────────────────────── -Achievements is a system that displays situational objectives and grant rewards based on the level of completion. [Achievements Classification]
-Achievements are classified into following categories, and the list is organized by each category. ◆Item
-Specific Item Usage and Weapon Repair Achievements
◆Dungeon
-Dungeon Level and Time Attack Achievements
◆Battle
-Monster Hunt and Maximum Combo Achievements
◆Quest
-Main and Sub Quests Completion Achievements
◆Play
-Reaching levels, Playtime, and earning Gold Achievements
◆Community
-Community Related Achievements
◆Raid
-Raid Related Achievements [Achievements UI]
-Achievements UI is displayed by pressing the Trophy button from the quickmenu or by the Shortcut【L】 can be used to display the UI.

-With Achievement UI, you can receive and look at the progress of Achievements categorically. [Achievement Progression]
-Achievement Progression will initially have the first level achievements unlocked, and the next level of achievements will unlock as the previous levels are completed. [Achievement Completion]
-When achievements are complete, notification will be displayed. With the Achievement UI, rewards can be received by clicking on the "Receive Rewards" button.
