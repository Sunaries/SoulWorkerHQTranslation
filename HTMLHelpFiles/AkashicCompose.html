[Synthesis]
────────────────────────────────── - Sythesis allows players to increase the experience of an AR Card they own. In order to perform synthesis, another AR Card is consumed as material. - When performing synthesis, the AR Card that was used as material is consumed and cannot be recovered. - In order to perform synthesis, you must own the AR Card at that time. - Please note that if AR cards from the Li event are used, all experience gained from synthesis prior to this will be reset.
