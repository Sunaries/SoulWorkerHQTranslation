[Socket]
────────────────────────────────── - Selected few weapon items have sockets where you can equip familias.
- The sockets on the weapons are either classified into activated sockets or deactivated sockets.
- You can equip familias to the activated sockets.
- Deactivated sockets can be activated by using the items that activate sockets. [Equipping Familias] - You can equip familias by right clicking the target weapon item and clicking Equip Socket on the menu that appears.
- You can drag and drop the familias that you have to the popup socket inventory, and then click Equip Familia to equip Familia to your weapons. 　 [Activating Sockets] - Socket activation can be done through Enhancement NPC 'Trisha' at the village. If you click the deactivated socket slot, the required item information to activate the slot will be displayed.
-If you click Add New Socket button while you have the item, you can activate the selected socket.
- Socket adding items can be purchased at the Item Mall.
